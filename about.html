About PHX MistMaster

Locally owned. Expert installed. Keeping Phoenix cool since 2014.

Our Story

PHX MistMaster was founded by Phoenix natives who understand what it means to live in extreme heat. After years of working in HVAC and outdoor cooling systems, we saw a gap in the market — homeowners and businesses needed reliable, high-quality misting systems installed by people who actually understand desert conditions.

Today, we've installed over 500 systems across the Valley, from backyard patios in Gilbert to restaurant terraces in Old Town Scottsdale. Every system we install is custom-designed for the specific space, using commercial-grade components that stand up to Arizona's intense sun and hard water.

We're not a franchise or a big box retailer — we're your neighbors. We answer our own phones, show up on time, and stand behind every installation with a comprehensive warranty.
